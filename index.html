    INTUITIVE CONTROLS IN A COMPACT BODY

    The X-T10 has a full set of controls in its compact body. Its design allows you to shoot in Program, Shutter Speed Priority, or Manual by adjusting the Shutter Speed Dial with aperture settings. For a Delicate exposure control, you can use the 1/3EV steps on the 3-stop exposure compensation dial for delicate exposure control.

    AUTO MODE

    Access the top plate's Auto Mode Switch lever to switch the camera into Advanced SR Auto Mode. It Automatically selects the optinum shooting settings from 58 pre-set scenes, so you can capture premium quality photos with ease

    DESIGN
    COMPACT AND LIGHTWEIGHT BODY

    With a camera body that weighs just 381g, the XT-10 has been designed to be compact to maintain the optimum balance between widht and height.

    ENHANCED OPERABILITY

    Three precision-milled aluminum dials gives the camera a premium feel and allows users to intuitively ajust the aperture, shutter speed, and shooting functions while taking a picture.

    920K-dot LCD

    The X-T10 has a 3.0-inch 920k-dot LCD monitor that can be moved up and down for high-and-low angl shots.

    EXCELLENT GRIP

    The grip's size and shape were carefully designed to enable an assured grip despite the camera's compact size.


    BUILT IN FLASH

    A stylish built-in flash is positioned in the center of the top plate.

    TWO COLOR OPTIONS

    Available in premium silver or sporty black, choose the color that best suits your style.


    HIGH-SPEED AUTO FOCUS SYSTEM

    Capture your subject perfectly with the new Autofocus System High-speed AF with on-board phase detection pixels.

    Capture subjects with speed and clarity using the camera's Intelligent Hybrid AF system that delivers autofocusing of up to .006 seconds. A new algorithm has been developed to enhance the camera's performance in low light and low contrast conditions.


    SIX DIFFERENT AF MODES


    AF-S + SINGLE POINT

    This feature delivers highly accurate autofocus on a specific area.

    AF-S + ZONE

    Effectively focus on a subject with moderate movements,which the Single Area mode may have difficulty capturing.

    AF-S + WIDE/TRACKING

    The camera automatically selects and focuses using several of the 77-point focus areas, which is suited to subjects with unpredictable movements or multiple moving subjects


    A more accurate performance

    The single-point AF divides the focus area into smaller sections to determine the distance to the subject for greater focusing accuracy.



    Eye Detection AF produces more beautiful portraits

    Automatically detect human faces with Face Detection and ensure accurate focus on human eyes with Eye Detection AF.

    AUTO MACRO MODE
    Continuous shooting with AF-C at 8.0 fps and advanced subject motion prediction

    This X-T10 features an advanced subject motion technology to predict the subject's movenebt using previous data, which enables continuous shooting with AF-C*5 at up to 8.0 fps.


    Auto Macro for easy close-up photography

    The Auto Macro function eliminates pressing the Macro button before shooting close-ups while maintaining the conventional AF speed


    IMAGE QUALITY
    Fujifilm has over 80 years of perfecting the skill of color reproduction, which lives on in the X-T10. A wide dynamic range combined with the ability to record the diversity of white balance conditions result in pleasing color output and picture clarity.
