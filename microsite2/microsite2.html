LHF X-20

Lens Hood, Hood Cap and Special Protector Filter 3pc Set (available in silver or black).

features
  • The 3-item accessory set consists of a lens hood with a slit to avoid blocking the the viewfinder view, a hood cap and a protector specially treated with a multi-layer Super EBC (Electron Beam Coating) for improved resistance to ghosting and lens flare.
  • Available in silver or black you can select the set that matches the color and design of your X20
  • Filter case and hood pouch are boundled.

    • note

    Attach the adapter ring AR-X100 / LH-X100 exclusively designed for X100S / X100, then attach the filter.
